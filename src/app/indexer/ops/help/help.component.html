Team Operations FaQ

This tool allows you to share a command overview with your teammates.

1. You can share commands using your in-game command overview (Curator Advisor required).
2. Your uploaded commands are shared with the teams that you are currently contributing to.
3. On this page, you and your teammates will see a real-time combined command overview.


Use our userscript to share your in-game command overview

If you want to start or contribute to an operation, simply upload your commands using the in-game userscript.
To do this, you need to have the userscript installed and you need to be part of a team.
