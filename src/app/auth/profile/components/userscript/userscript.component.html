Step 3: Link the script to your account

  • Open or refresh your game window after installing the script.
  • A popup will be displayed with an authentication link. Click on the link in the popup.
  • Sign in to your GrepoData account after clicking on the link. This will link the script to your personal account.

Done! You can now start collecting enemy intelligence.

Userscript functionality overview

Once you have installed the userscript, you can collect new intelligence by indexing reports from your inbox or alliance forum. While in-game, you will also be able to browse the collected intel for each town.


1. Indexing new reports

Use the 'Index +' button as shown on the right to upload the report to your GrepoData account.
The index button will be added to reports in your inbox and on reports posted on your alliance forum.
You can join a GrepoData team to share the collected intel with your allies.

2. Browsing collected intel

Use the 'Intel' button in the town context menu to open the town intelligence window.

In this window you will be able to see the collected intel for a specific town as shown on the right.
You will also be able to view an overview of player and alliance intel on grepodata.com
